WHO WE ARE

Founders Patricia and Pia grew up in the hustle and bustle of Metro Manila and sought solace on the weekends within the crystal blue waters just outside the city. The pair of sisters weaved that seaside mentality wherever they went– finding the perfect kaftan to pair with their swimsuits and rattan cutlery to complete their dining sets. Their own love aside, they wanted to create a line that encapsulates the ever-loved feeling of an island getaway. Cariloo was born out of the idea of capturing the essence of a Filipina’s tropical lifestyle wherever she goes. Whether it be ready-to-wear pieces, travel pouches or accents to style your home– Cariloo is designed to make every day feel like a warm embrace from the Philippine sun.
