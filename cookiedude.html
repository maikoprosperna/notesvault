What is a Half Baked Cookie?

Drag a block here

Cookie Dude’s Half-Baked Cookies are your favorite classic chocolate chip cookie, all levelled-up! Cookie Dude’s Half Baked Cookies are not your average chocolate chip cookie; they are HALF-BAKED! Not quite soft-baked or crispy, but a hybrid of the two; they are baked at just the right time and temperature to achieve a toasty, crispy exterior while the inside stays perfectly velvety soft and ooey-gooey. A medium rare cookie, if you will.
